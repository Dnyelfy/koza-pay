Send a recallable payment

The amount is encrypted in your browser before it ever touches the chain. The payment sits in escrow: the recipient can claim it — or you can recall it any time before they do. Observers see that a payment happened. They never see how much.

Confidential payroll

Pay up to 20 people in a single transaction. All amounts are encrypted together in one batch proof. Each recipient can only decrypt their own salary — coworkers can't see each other's amounts, and neither can anyone else.
